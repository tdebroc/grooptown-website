News & milestones

A decade of building & sharing

From gamified learning apps to enterprise data platforms and community engagement.

2025

Gamified learning application

Grooptown developed an application to help clients learn modern data tools in a gamified way — a great success with the teams who used it.

2024

Le Flambeau de la Vie

Grooptown sponsored "le Flambeau de la Vie," a symbolic race raising awareness for the importance of organ donation.

2023

Paris Apache Airflow Meetup

Thibaut spoke at the Paris Apache Airflow Meetup, sharing how he used and extended Airflow's best features to build an enterprise-grade data platform.

2022

AI Contest on CodingGame

A new twist on the annual AI contest — this year played on CodingGame's Clash of Code. A lot of fun, and a different kind of challenge for the students.

2021

Escape Game — Space Invaders

Built a playful escape game set in the universe of Flash Invader. Play it here →

2020

AI Competition on Robot Turtles

An impressive winning AI took home two drones. Congratulations to the students behind it — the battles were fierce.

2019

AI Contest on KingDomino

Four Parrot drones and Robot Turtles boardgames up for grabs. Fantastic AI battles and well-deserved winners.

2018

AI Contest on Risk

The yearly contest moved to Risk — played on both the classic map and on a Westeros (Game of Thrones) variant.

2017

AI Contest on Snorkuking · Night of Computer Science

An AI competition around the game Snorkuking — winners and finalists took home Parrot drones. Grooptown also sponsored the Nuit de l'Info, designing two challenges: an Easter Egg in a JS library and a recommendation-algorithm chatbot.

2016

Big Data Paris Meetup at ISEP

Grooptown hosted the Big Data Paris Meetup at ISEP. Thibaut gave a talk on the Hadoop ecosystem, presented as a playful advent calendar.

2015

Launch of the Artificial Intelligence Contest at ISEP

Grooptown launched its annual AI contest at ISEP: students submit their own AIs that send requests to a central server, with live matches displayed on the amphitheater big screen. Parrot drones for the winners.

2015 – 2020

Wave App

Built Wave, an interactive learning platform where students complete exercises, give feedback to peers, and progress through a fully gamified environment.
